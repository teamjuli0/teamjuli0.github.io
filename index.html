ABOUT ME
LIKE THERE'S MUCH TO KNOW

Now that i have your attention, let me formally introduce myself. My name is Julio Valdez, but I go by Lilo. It’s actually kinda funny because my last pet was a puppy named Stitch (for a completely different reason). I grew up in El Monte, CA with mom and 3 brothers as well as spending lots of time with my dad who lives in Boyle Heights, CA before settling in with my wife and son in the DownTown Los Angeles area. Most of my time at moms house was spent learning about smartphones and tablets and discovering the versatility found in the Google/Android ecosystem as well as digging through root files of an old iphone 5s to accomplish a high score on flappy bird that none of my friends or family could beat. At dads house however, my days consisted heavily on hours of playing Call of Duty: Modern Warfare 2. And as any honest, hardcore fan of the franchise would do, I started looking into different ways to hack my profile. After finding a mod menu which I really enjoyed using, I attempted to dissect it and learn from it to create my own version (to my own surprised it worked! Sorta…)

I guess what I’m trying to say is that some people may know more code than myself, but I like to think that my biggest strength is my ability to learn. The biggest reason for that is that I’m honestly passionate about coding in general and when someone wants something, they do whatever it takes to get it. So if you’re looking for an expert at any specific language, I’m not your guy. But if you’re looking for someone who is willing to learn and work at accomplishing your goals just as hard as you do, then look no further, I'm your guy.
